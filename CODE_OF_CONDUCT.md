# Code Of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, respectful, and specific.
- Discuss research claims with reproducible evidence.
- Separate workflow/tooling issues from investment claims.
- Respect platform terms, data licenses, and privacy boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Publishing credentials, cookies, session states, private platform exports, or another
  user's research records.
- Presenting generated factors or backtests as guaranteed returns.
- Harassment, personal attacks, or repeated off-topic promotion.
- Encouraging misuse of external platforms, rate limits, or access controls.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove issues, discussions, pull requests, or comments that expose
private data or violate these rules. Repeated violations may lead to blocking from the
project.
